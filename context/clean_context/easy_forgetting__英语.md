# 英语  
## 写作句式  
1. 抬头
- 写给机构: To whom it may concern  
1. 写信内容
- 自我介绍/写信目的/背景与自己的态度
- 信的内容、原因
- 再次表明目的，寄语  
1. 投诉信
- I venture to write to complain about the quality of my iPhone which ...
- As the product is under warranty, I would like to exchange it for another one or get a refund.
1. 建议信
- I'm a faithful reader of your newspaper and I'd like to convey in this letter my deepest concern about ...
- It gives me great pleasure to extend my warm welcome to your coming visit to our campus on behalf of Students' Union.
- It's a great pleasure that this letter has found its way to your attention, to appeal on the issue of ...
- I propose the following measures to be taken both to ... and to ...
- I have received your letter and I'm writing here to express my gratification for your congratulation on my success.
- Practice makes perfect. Translation is a kind of competence that needs years of practice, so you need patience to improve your translation skills.
1. 道歉信
- I'm writing to make my apology to you for ...
- It was most embarrassing to discover that ...
- Sorry again for any inconvenience I have caused.
1. 辞职信
- I'm very grateful to be employed by you as ...
- I appreciate the oppotunity of having working here
- I highly appreciate the invaluable experience working here
- I decide to quit this job for something else that conforms to my former preparation
- Please accept my sincere apologies for any inconveniences my leaving may cause
- I do appreciate your kind consideration you have shown me
1. 求职信
- I am responding to your ad on the newspaper **for the position of**
- It prompts me to offer you my qualifications because your requirements closely parallel my working experience
- I wish you would give me an oppotunity to be interviewed. I can be reached at ...
- Any favorable consideration of my application will be greatly appreciated
1. 推荐信
- I bet you will love this and wish you enjoy this
- Any favorable consideration to this letter would be considered a personal favor  
1. 感谢信
- I was a total stranger when arriving in this city, even ... It is you who have generous help ...
- With deepest gratitude I write to you here a few lines
1. 祝贺信
- It's my great pleasure to congratulate you on ...
- I can well imagine how proud of your parents are now
- Because of our close association with you over the past 10 years, we know how well you are qualified for this important position
- We are delighted to see your true ability has finally won recognition
- May each succeeding year bring you great happiness and prosperity
1. 邀请信
- 要点: 表明目的 (邀请)、说明原因 (entailed)、提供时间/地点/联系方式
- I'm writing to invite you to be ...
- It will be our great honor if you would come to the competition. Your attention will make Friday evening a wonderful night.
- The contest will begin at 19:00 o'clock. I will wait for you at the center entrance.
- We would be looking forward to your participation in the party.
1. 咨询信
- Your prompt reply to this letter would be highly appreciated.
1. 告示
- 要点: 开门见山介绍要点（时间地点联系方式）
1. 备忘录
- 形式: 电子邮件 (TO, FROM, SUBJECT, DATE)  
1. 表达歉意
- 非正式: Sorry again for any inconvenience I have caused.
- 正式: Please accept my sincere apologies for any inconveniences my leaving may cause  
---  
- Relishing expressions
- In the following days == In the days that followed
- With the rapid increase of economy == The boosting economic development in China sadly witnessed
- fade == deteriorate
- drop == shun  
- Replacement for `As far as I am concerned`
- Through the artistic representation, we can be easily reminded of a popular phenomenon in the society, that is, ...  
- Phrases
- 认真负责: Cautious and conscientious  
## 短文写作  
1. 第一段
- 现象型
- It has been a common phenomenon for years, if not decades, that ...
- ... sparkles in China nowadays.
- ... has become an increasingly serious problem that most people, especially the kids, who are exposed the most to ..., in the network age nowadays.
- 道理型
- This cartoon is elaborated, mainly to illustrate that ... which exerts huge impacts on our daily life.  
1. 第二段
- The photo does reflect a thought-provoking social phenomenon which is not uncommon in China now.
- Superficially, it seems to be somewhat reasonable, but when weighing in the mind, we find there is an apparent tendency underlying this phenomenon, that is: ...  
1. 第三段
- 字数不够，举例来凑: Take us for an example, ...  
## 翻译  
| 种类                     | 策略               |
|--------------------------|--------------------|
| 长句，无逗号             | 断句、翻译、重读   |
| 长句，大量逗号           | 理清逻辑、更改顺序 |
| 长句，大量逗号，大量术语 | 原顺序翻译         |
| 短句，无逗号             | 剥洋葱，加逗号     |  
1. 先出主语
- Although Gutman admits that ... → 古特曼尽管认为
- Because it is slippery, it is used for lubrication → 因为它非常润滑，所以可以用作润滑剂  
1. 一个原则: 主谓结构和偏正结构可互换  
1. 插入语在中间用破折号 (pp. 26)  
1. 被动语态，无宾语改主动，有宾语可用其他词替代: 受、为所、由、让、可以，抑或省略  
1. 被动变主动: in ways not fully understood → 他们自己也没有完全理解其方式  
1. some kind of → 某些  
1. 中文先事实后评论，英文先评论后事实，需更改语序  
1. 善用本位词 (这、这一切、其)，可以指代与原文意思不同的东西 (句子)，但逻辑意思要清楚  
1. 句首时间用状语: A farmer of 1800 could ... → 一个农民在 1800 年，能 ...  
1. 长句主语: Achieving ... (sub) requires ... → 要想 ..., 这需要  
1. 偏正互换: This treasury of silt → 宝贵的泥沙; Europeans of today → 当今的欧洲人  
1. 换主语/主谓搭配: Petrol has a low boiling point → 汽油的沸点低  
1. 代词指明要点: What remains → 剩下的 *东西*  
1. 动词分配原则: It is not known that A, or B. → 人们不知道 A, 也不知道 B。  
1. 被认为 → 可以视作: the resemblance is regarded as compelling envidence → 这种类似可视作是极有说服力的证据  
1. 同位语翻译
- 主谓结构: Beijing, the capital of China, is beautiful. → 北京是中国的首都，它很美丽
- 顶真/重译法: We head for the same goal, the goal of ... → 我们向相同目标前进，这个目标是 ...  
1. fact
- The fact is → 事实上 (插入语)
- The fact that → 可省略翻译，直接翻译主语从句  
1. 动词的过渡现象: resort to compromise and capitulation → 妥协投降 (resort to 为过渡动词，可省略)  
1. 有动词词根的抽象名词译为动词: ruthlessly opression of the people → 镇压人民  
1. 代词译法: 具体化 (增词)、有详有略  
1. 形容词副词译法
- 使用延伸含义: thoughtful → 体贴
- 相互转换 (形容词+形容词根式名词 → 副词+名词): I was flushed with childish pleasure → 我天真地感到高兴
- 单独翻译: try to piece the broken vase together in vain → 试着把花瓶碎片拼在一起，却怎么也拼不起来  
1. 中文定语的排序: 后置形容词 + 前置形容词 + 后置名词 + 前置名词 + 中心词  
1. 范畴词按大的方面来说: how near the harbor was → 海湾有多远  
1. 同指译法: He is a rich man → 他很富有 (he 和 man 不重复翻译)  
同指现象: I succeeded in making the letters correctly → 我把单词写对了 (不重复翻译)  
1. When 的译法: 当 ... 时/ ..., 这时  
1. 独立主格结构翻作主谓结构:
Their galabias and turbans stained by the sweat and dirt of a long day's work, they sat in front of a wayside shop
→ 他们干了一天的活，长袍上、头巾上，又是汗，又是土，坐在路边商店的门口  
1. 长主语翻译: 主语单独翻译，形容词、副词、定语可单独翻译，然后使用本位词代替之前的主语
- A long-held view of the history of English colonies that became the United States has been that ...
→ 长期以来，有这样一种关于英国殖民地历史的观点，这块殖民地后来变成了美国，这种观点是 ...
- The resounding success of Cucarao experiment → 库卡索岛上的试验取得了巨大的成功，这 ...
